General Instructions

  • Section A (Objective): 5 questions × 1 mark each.

  1. Ampere

  4. Ohm

  2. Velocity

  4. Speed

  1. Temperature of the conductor is constant

Section C — Subjective (5 Marks each)  |  5 × 5 = 25

11. A particle starts from rest and moves with uniform acceleration of 2 m s−2. Determine its velocity after 8 s and the distance covered in this interval. Show all steps and formulae.

[5 marks]

12. A 5 kg block is lifted vertically through 3 m at constant speed. Calculate the work done against gravity. If the lift takes 2 s, compute the power developed. Take g=9.8 m s−2.

[5 marks]

13. Two resistors, 4 Ω and 12 Ω, are connected (a) in series and (b) in parallel across 12 V. For each case, find the equivalent resistance and the current drawn from the source.

[5 marks]

14. An object is placed 30 cm in front of a convex lens of focal length 15 cm. Using the lens formula, find the image position and magnification. State the nature of the image.

[5 marks]

15. A tuning fork of frequency 512 Hz produces sound in air where the speed of sound is 340 m/s. Calculate the wavelength. Explain how frequency and wavelength change (if at all) when the sound enters a medium where its speed is higher.

[5 marks]
